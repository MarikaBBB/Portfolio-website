About Me

Education: Having studied at the Academy of Art in Venice and the University of Padua, I've gained a strong foundation in the History of Art. My experiences from Venice's artistic corridors to the digital landscape of London have reinforced my appreciation for the convergence of art and technology.

Museums, Leadership, and Storytelling: At the Museum of Freemasonry, my role evolved from interpreting artefacts to taking on leadership responsibilities, highlighting my ability to manage teams and streamline operations. My storytelling skills and leadership acumen truly shone in this capacity. At Sir John Soane's Museum, I created and led an immersive multi-sensorial tour showcasing my commitment to offering exceptional visitor experiences. Bridging the worlds of museum storytelling and technology, I've engaged with platforms like Google Art & Culture, underscoring my dedication to enriching audience experiences using digital tools.

Research and Collection Management: I've been involved in several notable projects like the HLF Digitisation Project at The Courtauld Institute of Art. I'm particularly enthusiastic about utilizing tools like Index+ to refine collection management, making it more efficient and accessible. At the Cutty Sark, I introduced a system in Excel to effectively catalog previously unsorted archival materials, indicating my proactive approach to problem-solving.

Software Development and Training: I possess foundational knowledge in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Python. My coding journey began with courses from Code First: Girls and further honed by platforms like OdinProject and FreeCodeCamp, reflecting my continuous drive for learning.

The Road Ahead: As I pivot towards a career in software engineering, my background in cultural understanding and leadership equips me uniquely for the challenges ahead. My aim is to become a software engineer where my managerial expertise complements my growing technical skills. I'm determined to use these combined strengths to foster team collaborations, develop innovative solutions, and always prioritise user needs and business goals.
