About Me

Education: I have studied at the Academy of Art in Venice and the University of Padua, gaining a solid foundation in the History of Art. My experiences from Venice's artistic corridors to the digital landscape of London have deepened my appreciation for the intersection of art and technology.

Background: With a passion for creating engaging digital experiences that make a positive impact, I pursued my interest in technology and storytelling through roles in the museum sector. I bring a unique blend of experiences in art history, museum administration, and leadership. In my museum roles, I led projects to enhance visitor experiences both onsite and online.

Working in museums during challenging times taught me valuable lessons in effectively engaging audiences online and designing user-friendly digital interfaces. I also worked on projects to digitize and catalog historical artifacts using collection management systems. This hands-on experience showed me the power of software and databases to organize information and make it accessible.

Coding Journey: Eager to better understand the technology behind the museum software I had used, I began teaching myself web development through online courses and tutorials. With each new language and framework I learned, I became more and more captivated by the creativity and problem-solving involved in coding.

Goals: Now, as I look to transition into a full-time software engineering role, I'm excited to combine my technical skills with my background in delivering compelling narratives and designing accessible digital experiences.

As a developer, I want to build web and mobile applications that engage users and enhance understanding - whether it's of museums or any other subject matter. My desire is to create software that informs, inspires, and empowers people.

Soft Skills: In my previous roles managing both people and projects, I honed my collaboration and communication abilities. I'm a proactive team player who enjoys working cross-functionally to bring ideas to life. Technical challenges energise me, and I'm always looking to expand my coding skills and stay up-to-date on new technologies.

Growth: I'm enthusiastic about bringing my passion for coding and my collaborative nature to projects that make a genuine positive impact on society. I'm eager to join a dynamic team where I can contribute my unique perspective while rapidly growing as a developer.
