About Me

Education: I have studied at the Academy of Art in Venice and the University of Padua, gaining a solid foundation in the History of Art. My experiences from Venice's artistic corridors to the digital landscape of London have deepened my appreciation for the intersection of art and technology.

Museums, Leadership, and Storytelling: At the Museum of Freemasonry, my responsibilities expanded from interpreting artifacts to assuming leadership roles, demonstrating my ability to manage teams and streamline operations. At Sir John Soane's Museum, I designed and led an immersive multi-sensory tour, showcasing my dedication to providing exceptional visitor experiences. I collaborated with Google Arts & Culture and imagineear on digital projects, merging museum storytelling with technology. This work enhanced my ability to convey complex information engagingly and deepened my skills in creating inclusive user experiences while effectively teaming up with different departments and external teams.

Research and Collection Management: I have been involved in notable projects such as the HLF Digitization Project at The Courtauld Institute of Art. I am particularly enthusiastic about using tools like Index+ to improve collection management, making it more efficient and accessible. At the Cutty Sark, I implemented an Excel-based archival system integrated with TRIM to effectively organize previously unsorted archival materials, exhibiting my proactive problem-solving approach.

Software Development and Training: I possess foundational knowledge in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Python. I began my coding journey with courses from Code First Girls and further improved my skills through platforms like OdinProject and FreeCodeCamp, showcasing my continuous drive for learning.

The Road Ahead: As I transition into a career in software engineering, my cultural understanding and leadership background uniquely prepare me for the challenges ahead. My goal is to become a software engineer, leveraging my managerial expertise to complement my expanding technical skills. I am determined to utilize these combined strengths to foster team collaborations, develop innovative solutions, and always prioritize user needs and business goals.
